Overview of the Requirements

The Accessibility Standard for Employment requires that employers make their workplaces accessible by taking action in the following seven areas:

  1. Inform employees about accessible employment practices
  2. Meet employees’ communication needs
  3. Create individualized accommodation plans, on request of an employee
  4. Take accessibility into account during performance management, career development, and redeployment processes
  5. Establish a return to work process
  6. Ensure the safety of employees with disabilities
  7. Practice thorough recordkeeping, maintain confidentiality, and complete training on accessible employment
